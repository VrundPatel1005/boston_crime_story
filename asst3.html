Boston Shooting Incidents: Where and When

This interactive data story explores shooting-related incidents in Boston using the Boston Open Data API. The first chart shows which districts report the most shooting incidents, highlighting areas such as B2, B3, and C11. The second chart shows the number of shootings at each hour of the day, revealing that incidents peak in the evening and nighttime hours. Together, these charts provide insights into both the geographic concentration and temporal patterns of shootings in Boston.

Data Integrity Note: All axes are fixed to the city-wide maximum to maintain a consistent scale. "Shooting" status (1) includes both confirmed injuries and ballistics evidence found.
Source: Boston Open Data Portal | Visualizations: Plotly.js | CSS/Design: Google Gemini |
